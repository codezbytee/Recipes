Beef Tacos

Description

Quick skillet beef tacos with a flavorful, lightly seasoned beef and fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 small tortillas
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, diced onions, salsa, sour cream, chopped cilantro, lime wedges

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add seasoning and water. Simmer 5â€“7 minutes, until sauce thickens slightly.
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble tacos: beef, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, salsa
