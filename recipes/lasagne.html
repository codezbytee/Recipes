Lasagne

Description

A cozy, easy lasagna with a hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melty mozzarella layered between noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 15oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). If using regular noodles, cook them according to package directions; if using no-boil noodles, skip this step. Brown the ground beef in a skillet, drain fat, add garlic (if using) and the marinara sauce; season with salt and pepper. Simmer 5–10 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, ¼ cup Parmesan, and a pinch of salt.
  3. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Layer with noodles, then spread the ricotta mixture, sprinkle a portion of mozzarella, and repeat. Top with remaining sauce and the rest of the mozzarella and Parmesan.
  4. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes. Uncover and bake 10 minutes more, until bubbling and cheese is melted. Let stand 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with parsley if desired and serve with a side salad or garlic bread.
